Announcement and call for papers: 3rd Workshop on Robots in Clutter - Perception and Interaction in Clutter

IROS 2014, Chicago, Illinois, Sept. 18, 2014

We are organising the 3rd Workshop on Robots in Clutter in conjunction with IROS 2014 in Chicago. Deadline for submission of papers and extended abstracts is July 25th (extended!).

Check the webpage http://workshops.acin.tuwien.ac.at/clutter2014 for more details.

July, 21 - 25, 2014: 1st SQUIRREL Summer School in Freiburg

July, 21 - 25, 2014 in Freiburg

This year's summer school feataures excellent invited lectures: Tamim Asfour, KIT; Tom Duckett, Univ. of Lincoln; Cyrill Stachniss, Univ. of Freiburg (now Bonn); and Paul Furgale, ETHZ). Guided by an initial workshop on software integration participants will then put their knowledge to use in hands-on work on our Robotino robots to solve an actual simplified clean-up task.
